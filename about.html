About Feather Academy

At Feather Academy, we believe in developing intellectual brilliance and technical mastery by achieving a balanced combination of academic excellence and experience. We are one of the top institutes in NEET, JEE and Technical Education and offer a rich learning experience where education is not based on exams but on conceptualising, innovating and succeeding in their respective fields.

Our Vision

For over 9 years, Our vision is to empower learners with holistic education that nurtures both academic proficiency and technological skills.

Our vision is to create future-ready personalities who can face the competitive examination and industry landscape with confidence, creativity as well as critical thinking.

Who We Are

Feather Academy is based on the ideals of innovation, integrity, and excellence, and it fills the gap between conventional academic coaching and modern technical education. Our faculty is of quality teachers, graduates and trainers who are certified of industry qualifications and are committed to helping students to intellectual autonomy and professional capacity.

Combining NEET, JEE, and higher technical education, we will enable students to further their scientific base, as well as gain world-relevant technical skills that can boost their academic and career paths.
